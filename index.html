Tribute to Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist widely known for his theory of relativity and his formula E=mc^2. He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.

Einstein is considered one of the greatest scientists in history, and his work has had a profound impact on the field of physics. His contributions to science and humanity are immeasurable.
